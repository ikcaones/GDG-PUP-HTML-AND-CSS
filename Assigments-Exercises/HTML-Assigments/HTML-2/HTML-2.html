Ian Kylle Caones (Naizone)

Introduction

Ian Kylle Caones is a second-year BS Computer Engineering student at PUP Manila, a freelance graphic designer, and an aspiring web developer. His passion for design began in elementary school, and he now aims to bring his talent to the forefront as he enters the tech community. He aspires to create innovative and functional websites that not only perform efficiently but also feature engaging and visually appealing designs.

Experiences

Ian has been contributing to his community since high school and continues to do so in college. He has assisted his teachers in major school events, served as a layout artist for several student-led publications, and strengthened his commitment to volunteerism by leading graphics committees for various major events at PUP Manila. Now, he is expanding his expertise as a freelancer, having founded his own creative studio, Naizone, in late 2024. This venture marks the beginning of a new chapter in his journey as a graphic designer, where he looks forward to embracing exciting opportunities in the near future.

Tools

The tools Ian used are mainly Adobe Photoshop for layouting, Adobe Illustrator for illustrations and creating of vector objects, and Figma for added convenience.
